Hello!

I am currently working for Entangled networks and Prof. Christopher Wilson developing long-range interactions in superconducting qubits. The position is funded jointly by Entangled networs and Mitacs accelerate program.

I recieved my PhD in Physics-Quantum Information from Institute for Quantum Computing, University of Waterloo in October 2019 under the supervision of Prof. Raymond Laflamme.
